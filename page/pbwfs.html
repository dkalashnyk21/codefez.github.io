Python Beginners Workbook for Secondary Schools


A free workbook for your students

Created in Nov 2013 the workbook has been downloaded over 10,000 times.

A simple introduction to most concepts in programming through the Python language.

Python Beginners Workbook for Secondary Schools has been created with a Share-Alike Licence in order to help teach the basics to students of all abilities.

The idea is to get students to practice learning concepts using worksheets that can be printed out in order to help encourage confidence in programming. When studying maths or other similarly taught subjects, students are given repeated opportunities to practice simple equations to develop a skill. The idea behind this book is of a similar approach.
